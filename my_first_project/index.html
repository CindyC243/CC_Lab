What we know so far about Cognitron Infestation: The Lumisquirm is a fascinating creature found on the planet Echrovia. It appears as a series of interconnected segments, resembling a worm-like creature. Each segment is adorned with vibrant colors, with each segment potentially having a different hue, creating a mesmerizing display as it moves. The Lumisquirm's head segment is distinguishable by its slightly larger size and is adorned with two luminous eyes that emit a soft glow. These eyes help the creature navigate its environment and locate its prey. Its mouth, located on the underside of the head segment, is surrounded by a circular array of sensory organs, aiding in detecting nearby food sources. As the Lumisquirm moves, it propels itself forward with rhythmic contractions of its body segments.The Lumisquirm gets its energy source from yellow-looking creatures and those creatures come in two colors, yellow and red. Yellow is food, red is poison.
